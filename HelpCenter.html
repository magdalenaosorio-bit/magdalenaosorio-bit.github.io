HELP CENTER

What is the SheSafe women's center?

The SheSafe shelter is a help and protection center available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, dedicated to providing comprehensive care to women, girls, and children under 12 who are in situations of risk, violence, or vulnerability.

The shelter offers a safe, confidential, and dignified space where individuals can receive psychological support, basic medical care, food, beds for resting, and access to basic services such as hygiene, water, and clothing. Additionally, it has trained staff to provide human accompaniment during the recovery process.

PURPOSE

The purpose of SheSafe is to protect and support women, girls, and children under 12, providing them with a safe place where they can feel sheltered and receive the necessary care to recover their physical and emotional well-being.

Likewise, it seeks to help people rebuild their lives through professional accompaniment, emotional support, and access to basic resources that allow them to move forward toward a safer, more stable life free of violence.
