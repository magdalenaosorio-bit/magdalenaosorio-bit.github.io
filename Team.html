SheSafe nació de la necesidad de recuperar nuestra tranquilidad al movernos por la ciudad. No somos solo una app de transporte; somos una red de apoyo diseñada por y para nosotras.

Who are we?

We are SheSafe, a company made for women, by women.
We founded our company based on the belief that women need a safe haven from the world that can be unsafe at every turn.
Our goal with our company is to be able to offer women a chance to feel safe and understood, even for just a moment.
