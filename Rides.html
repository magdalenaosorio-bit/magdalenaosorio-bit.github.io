RIDES

What are rides like with the SheSafe app?

The SheSafe transportation service is designed exclusively for women, girls, and children under 12 years of age, offering a safer and more reliable mobility alternative. All drivers are women and go through a detailed verification process.

The service is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, providing accompaniment and maximum safety during every ride.

PURPOSE

The purpose of the SheSafe service is to provide a safe, reliable, and accessible means of transportation for women and minors, creating a space where passengers can feel comfortable and protected at all times.

Furthermore, a significant part of the profits generated by the service goes towards funding the SheSafe help center, allowing the support line and psychological care to be maintained.
