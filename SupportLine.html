SUPPORT LINE

How does our support line work?

The SheSafe support line is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, to provide immediate assistance in situations of emergency, risk, or vulnerability.

The service offers real-time accompaniment, dispatch of patrols and emergency personnel to the user's location, as well as guidance throughout the situation to ensure greater safety and support.

It also features psychological assistance through calls with trained professionals to provide emotional support at any time.

PURPOSE

The purpose of the SheSafe support line is to offer protection, support, and immediate response to users when they need it most. It seeks to provide safety and peace of mind through rapid assistance, constant accompaniment, and emotional support, ensuring that no person has to face a situation of risk or crisis alone.
