Frequently asked questions

What safety features are available during a ride?

Your safety is our priority. Our app allows you to share your real-time location with trusted contacts. Every trip is GPS-monitored 24/7 by our support team, and the app includes a panic button that connects you directly with local authorities and our specialized Support Center.

What services does the Support Center provide?

The Support Center is a safe haven offering psychological counseling, legal guidance, and crisis intervention for women in vulnerable situations. You can access these resources via the "Help" section in the app or by visiting our physical location. These services are available to all women in the community, regardless of ride history.

How is my personal data and privacy protected?

We take privacy seriously. We only share the minimum information required to complete a ride (first name and pickup location). Phone numbers are always masked, and all records within our Support Center are handled under strict confidentiality protocols to protect your identity.

Can I schedule a ride in advance?

Absolutely! We know how important it is to be on time for essential appointments. You can schedule rides up to 7 days in advance to ensure a driver is available exactly when you need to head to the Support Center, a doctor’s office, or any other destination.

What should I do if I feel unsafe or need immediate help outside of a ride?

If you are in immediate danger, use the Emergency Alert feature in the app. If you are not currently in a vehicle but need emotional or legal assistance, our Support Center operates a 24/7 crisis hotline where professionals are ready to provide guidance and immediate support.

Can I request a specific driver or save "Favorite Drivers"?

While we assign the nearest available driver to ensure a quick response, we do offer a "Favorite Driver" feature. If you had a great experience and felt comfortable with a specific driver, you can add her to your favorites list. The app will prioritize sending your request to her whenever she is online and nearby, helping you build a long-term circle of trust.

We are always happy to answer your questions!
